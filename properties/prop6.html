Property Description

Marylebone Square is a rare opportunity to be part of the provenance of aristocratic estates in Central London. A contemporary take on a classic London mansion, it is an intuitively designed building that slots into the urban fabric of Marylebone. The residences are refined, elegant and intuitively designed; each apartment is generously spacious yet warmly intimate. Created by developers, Concord London and Simon Bowden Architects, all residences benefit from floor-to-ceiling windows and glass sliding doors giving each space a bright and capacious feel. Additional features include 24 hour concierge service. Marylebone Square has an estimated completion of 2021 and complies with a 10 year build warranty by NHBC.
